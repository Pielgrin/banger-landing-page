Data storage duration

Your data is retained until you delete it (see next section for deletion).

Delete information collected about you

I wanted to ensure that you have full control over your data collected via Quiz Rap. To delete all the information kept about you, you can follow the following steps:

  • Open Quiz Rap
  • On homepage, open the side menu by clicking on the button on the top left of the screen.
  • Click on the “Réinitialiser l’application” (“Reset the app”) button, which is the last button of the side menu.
  • A pop-up appears to confirm your choice.
  • Click on the “Réinitialiser” (“Reset”).
That’s it, your Unique Device Identifier and all your progress in the app are deleted from our databases.
